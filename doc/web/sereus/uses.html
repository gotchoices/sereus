Use Cases

Examples Sereus can enable (from the README):

  • Secure Messaging: Direct peer connections by consent; no central gatekeeper.
  • Social Media: User-curated timelines/feeds without opaque ranking.
  • Classifieds/Marketplaces: Neutral search, escrow, shipping services by choice.
  • Distributed Storage: Cadres shard and replicate user data across trusted nodes.
  • Medical Records: Patient-owned threads, provider access by QR/invite.
  • Community Voting: Transparency and verifiability for elections.
  • Digital Currency: MyCHIPs retooled for shared threads.
