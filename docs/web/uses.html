What Can You Build with Sereus?

Thread databases with arbitrary SQL schemas can support virtually any application. The consent-based, peer-to-peer foundation of Sereus opens up possibilities that are difficult or impossible to achieve with centralized platforms.

Here are some examples that showcase the unique capabilities of the Sereus architecture:

Direct peer-to-peer messaging without central brokers. Identity scoped to threads, no spam, privately operated relays.

Common Themes

Across all these use cases, certain patterns emerge:

  • User Control: Participants own their data and control who accesses it.
  • No Gatekeepers: Central platforms can't deplatform users or extract rent.
  • Privacy by Default: Identities and connections aren't publicly visible.
  • Consent Required: All interactions happen through explicit invitation and agreement.
  • Composable Services: Users can choose providers for different functions (search, storage, delivery) based on reputation.
